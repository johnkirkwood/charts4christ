Diagrams Info

The diagrams on the Diagrams page, marked PDF, are Adobe Acrobat (PDF) files. You will need the Acrobat Reader or the Adobe Acrobat application to view them. You may click on this link to go to the Adobe web site and download the free Reader. When you are ready to access a diagram, do one of the following.

Macintosh

  • Click the link. This will let you open the file if you have the Reader.
  • Click the link with the "Option" key held down. This will let you save the file to your own computer.

PC

  • Click the link. This will let you open the file if you have the Reader.
  • Right-click the link. This will let you choose to save the file to your own computer.

These instructions represent what should happen to the best of our information. Your experience may vary.

The diagrams marked JPEG should open in the browser in the usual way. If you want to keep a copy, just drag the image to your desk-top. That should create an icon and file on the desk-top which you can put away in the folder of your choice.
