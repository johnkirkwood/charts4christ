Intensely with all of my heart.
I yearn for the sound of your laughter
These days when we must be apart.

A Book Bound Verse

I looked into a book last night
And there on the printed page
I found all human feelings
Of love, of joy, of rage.
 
My heart reached out as I read the verse
To my love beyond the sea
To share with you the feelings strong
That grew and throbbed in me.
 
One poem that leaped from the leaf
As feelings that we share
A Barrett now a Browning wrote.
She mirrored how we care
 
Herewith, my dearest sweetest love,
I will repen her lines,
To tell you of deep love and true
That around our life entwines

 Go From Me

Go from me.
Yet I feel that I shall stand
Henceforward in thy shadow.
Nevermore alone upon the threshold of my door of individual life,
Shall I command the uses of my soul,
Nor lift my hand serenely in the sunshine as before,
Without the sense of that which I forbore:
Thy touch upon the palm.
The widest land doom takes to part us
Leaves thy heart in mine with pulses that beat double.
What I do and what I dream include thee,
As the wine must taste of its own grapes.
And when I sue God for myself,
He hears that name of thine
And sees within my eyes
The tears of two.

Elizabeth Barrett Browning,

Used here of necessity

A Gift

When thou dost quit thy country and thy kin
And leave familiar paths for this vast land
I hear thee speak, my love, of trust.
 
When thou dost bid thy fondest friends
A tearful au revoir,
I hear thee speak, sweet love, of grief.
 
And when thy nearest dearest kin
Are left a continent beyond thy farthest reach,
I hear thee speak, mon amie, of love.
 
Of all the given gifts
Of all the loves that warmed some briefest moment
Or stayed throughout two well lived lives,
Thou hast given of thyself the best.
And that so willingly and of the form
Of that which thou wouldst not.
Though pain and apprehension tremble all thou art,
The swiftness of thy words that said;
"I love.", "I will.", "I do."
Hath bared to me a heart so rare
That I can only pray my gift of all to thee
Doth speak of love so well.
