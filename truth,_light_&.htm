50 billion people in Israel?

If all the 50 billion or so people that ever lived on this earth are resurrected and are to be taught as Micah 4:2 states, there must be a way to hear and see other than being physically present to hear the truth message. Also, Micah 4:2 states that "the law shall go forth". By what means can the law "go forth"?

By radio frequency signals? A TV broadcast transmitter, in Israel, broadcasting a signal powerful enough to reach the other side of the earth would cause serious bodily harm to any living creature near it, in addition to sending most of its signal off to the stars instead of to Asia. No sane person is thinking of such a plan. To solve this problem, a digital signal could be distributed to many local TV stations around the world where the signal levels would be more manageable. However, this solution still omits any possibility of personal interaction with the message and searching or forwarding the program material is out of the question. The digital Internet has none of these problems.

A SONET for the Truth

SONET stands for Synchronous Optical NETwork. This is the system for the digital Optical transmission of data over the backbone or trunk lines that make up the internet. Light is guided by optical fibers as fine as human hair or less from Israel to all parts of the world. And from all parts of the world to Israel. And from all parts of the world to all other parts of the world. No other "broadcast" system can hope to do such networking! THIS is where light and truth come together.

Copyrights?

Will God Put limits and fetters on HIS truth which will "go forth"? NO! Copyrighting a work only serves to prohibit the distribution and/or alteration of the work unless a price is paid. Jesus paid, on the cross, the only price that will ever be put on the truth and light of God's word. The copyright notices that are seen in the front of many bibles are an affront to all that was FREELY given from God and an effort to levy a toll on a God given freeway so to speak.

Copyleft, which prohibits charging for the text or even its presentation and seeks to guarantee the fidelity of the author's thoughts and words, is much, much more appropriate for the truth and light that will flow so generously and lovingly through Israel from our heavenly father. It is in this spirit that all the contents of this site are attributed the copyleft status.

And speaking of copies, the digital media of the Internet permit and promote copies of copies to the nth generation with complete fidelity in every respect. The 10th generation copy of a QuickTime digital movie will have 100% of the quality it had in the original. The 10th generation copy of a VHS video tape will have lost most of its program and become mostly noise. This means that the truth and beauty of God's word will be maintained as we share it with our friends and as our friends share it with their friends ad infinitum.
